Admission

Admission is open for the 2025 academic session. Interested candidates are requested to follow the instructions below:

  • Fill out the online application form available on the college website.
  • Submit a copy of your SSC and HSC certificates.
  • Attach two passport-sized photographs.
  • Pay the admission fee through the specified bank.
  • Admission test dates and results will be announced on the website.
